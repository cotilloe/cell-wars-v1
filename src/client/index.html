    Cryptosis Game Play Instructions

    • Move your mouse on the screen to move your character.
    • Eat pellets and other players in order to grow your cell (food respawns every time a player eats it).
    • A player's mass is the number of pellets eaten pus the total mass of other players eaten.
    • Objective: Try to become the biggest cell in the game.
